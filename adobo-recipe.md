# Filipino Chicken Adobo

## Ingredients

- 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 1 cup water
- 1 head garlic, minced
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
- 1 tbsp cooking oil
- 1 tsp sugar (optional)
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

### 1. Marinate the Chicken

1. Combine the chicken, soy sauce, and half of the garlic in a bowl.
2. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.

### 2. Brown the Chicken

1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Remove the chicken from the marinade and brown it lightly on all sides.
3. Set aside the remaining marinade.

### 3. Simmer

1. Add the remaining garlic and sauté until fragrant.
2. Return the chicken to the pan.
3. Add the reserved marinade, vinegar, water, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
4. Bring to a boil without stirring for 2–3 minutes.

### 4. Cook Until Tender

1. Lower the heat and simmer for 30–40 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
2. Add sugar if desired.
3. Adjust seasoning with salt if needed.

### 5. Reduce the Sauce

1. Continue simmering until the sauce reaches your preferred consistency.

## Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with steamed rice.

## Tips

- For richer flavor, marinate overnight.
- Pork can be used instead of chicken.
- Some people like to fry the chicken briefly after simmering for extra texture.
